# MaiStorage Employee Handbook

## Company Overview

MaiStorage is a leading provider of cloud storage and data management solutions, founded in 2019 and headquartered in Singapore. We serve over 5,000 enterprise clients across the Asia-Pacific region with secure, scalable, and cost-effective storage infrastructure.

Our mission is to empower businesses with reliable data storage that scales seamlessly from gigabytes to petabytes. Our core products include MaiVault (enterprise backup), MaiSync (real-time file synchronization), and MaiArchive (long-term cold storage).

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave

Full-time employees are entitled to **18 days** of paid annual leave per calendar year. Part-time employees receive annual leave on a pro-rata basis calculated from their contracted hours.

Annual leave accrues monthly at a rate of 1.5 days per month. New employees may use accrued leave after completing their 3-month probation period.

Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in advance through the HR portal. Requests of 5 or more consecutive days require manager approval at least 2 weeks ahead.

Unused annual leave may be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year. Any carried-forward leave not used by March 31 will be forfeited.

### Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 14 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate (MC) is required for absences of 2 or more consecutive days.

Hospitalization leave of up to 60 days (inclusive of the 14 outpatient sick leave days) is provided with valid documentation.

### Parental Leave

Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 2 weeks of paid parental leave. Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption date.

### Compassionate Leave

Employees may take up to 3 days of paid compassionate leave for the passing of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, sibling).

## Product Specifications

### MaiVault (Enterprise Backup)

MaiVault provides automated, encrypted backup for enterprise data with the following specifications:

- **Storage tiers**: Hot (SSD), Warm (HDD), Cold (tape/archive)
- **Encryption**: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **RPO**: Recovery Point Objective of 15 minutes for hot tier
- **RTO**: Recovery Time Objective of 4 hours for hot tier, 24 hours for warm tier
- **Retention**: Configurable from 30 days to 10 years
- **Compliance**: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, PDPA
- **Maximum single file size**: 5 TB
- **Deduplication**: Inline block-level deduplication achieving 60-70% storage savings
- **Pricing**: From $0.023/GB/month (hot tier) to $0.004/GB/month (cold tier)

### MaiSync (Real-time Synchronization)

MaiSync enables seamless file synchronization across devices and teams:

- **Sync speed**: Delta sync with block-level differencing
- **Conflict resolution**: Automatic with version history (30-day default)
- **Platform support**: Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, Web
- **Maximum file size**: 50 GB per file
- **API rate limits**: 1,000 requests/minute per API key
- **Team sharing**: Granular permissions (view, edit, admin) with link sharing
- **Bandwidth optimization**: LAN sync detection, bandwidth throttling controls
- **Pricing**: From $8/user/month (Business) to $15/user/month (Enterprise)

### MaiArchive (Cold Storage)

MaiArchive is designed for long-term data preservation:

- **Durability**: 99.999999999% (11 nines)
- **Storage media**: Tape-backed with geographic redundancy (3 data centers)
- **Retrieval time**: Standard (12-24 hours), Expedited (1-4 hours at 3x cost)
- **Minimum storage duration**: 90 days (early deletion fees apply)
- **Immutability**: WORM (Write Once Read Many) compliance mode available
- **Lifecycle policies**: Automatic tiering from hot/warm to archive
- **Pricing**: $0.001/GB/month for standard retrieval

## IT Security Policy

### Password Requirements

All employees must adhere to the following password policy:
- Minimum 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Password rotation every 90 days
- No reuse of the last 10 passwords
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all systems

### Data Classification

Data is classified into four tiers:
1. **Public**: Marketing materials, public documentation
2. **Internal**: Internal memos, non-sensitive operational data
3. **Confidential**: Customer data, financial records, HR data
4. **Restricted**: Encryption keys, access credentials, security audit reports

### Incident Response

Security incidents must be reported to the IT Security team within 1 hour of discovery via the #security-incidents Slack channel or security@maistorage.com.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use my annual leave during the probation period?
**A:** No. Annual leave can only be used after completing the 3-month probation period. However, leave accrues from your start date, so you will have accrued days available once probation ends.

### Q: How do I request to work from home?
**A:** Submit a WFH request through the HR portal at least 1 day in advance. Your manager will approve or deny the request. Remember that core hours (10 AM - 4 PM) must be observed.

### Q: What happens to my leave if I resign?
**A:** Unused annual leave will be encashed at your daily rate in your final paycheck. Unused sick leave and compassionate leave are not encashable.

### Q: Is there a dress code?
**A:** MaiStorage follows a smart-casual dress code. Business attire is expected for client meetings. Jeans and sneakers are acceptable for regular office days.

### Q: How do I report a workplace concern?
**A:** You may report concerns to your direct manager, HR Business Partner, or anonymously through the Ethics Hotline at ethics@maistorage.com.

### Q: What is the company's policy on side projects?
**A:** Employees may pursue side projects outside of working hours provided they do not conflict with MaiStorage's business interests, use company resources, or involve proprietary information. Disclosure to your manager is recommended.

## Support Error Codes and Troubleshooting

The MaiStorage API and desktop clients return standardized error codes. Common codes:

- **ERR-4092** — Authentication token expired. Re-authenticate to obtain a fresh token.
- **ERR-5023** — Storage quota exceeded. The account has reached its provisioned capacity; upgrade the plan or free space.
- **ERR-6011** — API rate limit throttled. The client exceeded the allowed requests per minute; back off and retry.
- **ERR-7788** — File exceeds the maximum permitted size for the product tier.
- **ERR-8134** — Region temporarily unavailable; the request was routed to a failover region.
- **ERR-9001** — Internal server error. Retry with exponential backoff; if it persists, open a support ticket.

For persistent errors, contact support@maistorage.com with the error code and request ID.

## API and Network Configuration

The MaiStorage REST API is served from `https://api.maistorage.com/v2`.

- **Current API version**: v2.4 (released 2025). Version v1.9 is deprecated and reaches end-of-life on December 31, 2026.
- **Secure endpoint port**: TCP **8443** (TLS 1.3). The legacy cleartext port 8080 is disabled in production.
- **Webhook delivery**: up to 5 retry attempts with exponential backoff; endpoints must respond within 10 seconds.
- **Rate limit**: 1,000 requests per minute per API key (HTTP 429 with code ERR-6011 when exceeded).
- **Authentication**: OAuth 2.0 bearer tokens; tokens expire after 60 minutes (code ERR-4092 on expiry).
